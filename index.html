About the Band

Four souls, one sound

The PiriGuikis is a high-energy rock band born in 2018 from the underground venues of Madrid. Known for their explosive live performances and raw, unfiltered sound, they have toured across Spain and Europe, earning a devoted following.

Their music draws from decades of guitar-driven rock, fused with a modern intensity that keeps crowds coming back night after night.

Our Values

  • Community and connection
